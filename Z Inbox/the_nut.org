This is a tentative response to [[id:fb37c936-58b8-4ebe-8b1a-b7f9e86381c0][Enlightenment]] ideals and the elevation of [[id:0623dc01-9888-426f-a545-7b2b2ecddcc0][rationalism]] to the chief good of man's [[id:8a840ac7-5ee6-483a-bd77-ac21be95fcb6][being]]. I consider in some ways that this is a [[id:fd71b408-0b9c-49ad-bac6-c19abdb97149][Meta-Modernist]] response to both [[id:047c3922-2074-45b3-a197-6779a659a0a4][Modernism]] and [[id:7208df2b-b7d2-4434-89ef-8b5b079df472][Post-modernism]]. We cannot get back to [[id:21584b33-fe93-402c-a1c8-e1eccdad8370][pre-modernism]] without falling into the trap of [[id:db80d11e-ee51-49d9-8be0-10e51f52318b][Idealisation of the past]], this is done also from with a [[id:5f81bf60-3a42-4af1-9218-d4ef10c0985c][Reformed theological]] outlook.
